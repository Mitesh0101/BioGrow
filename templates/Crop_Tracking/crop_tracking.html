The "Squeeze Test": Dig down about 6 inches to root depth and grab a handful of soil. Squeeze it firmly.
  • Dry (Loose): Falls apart immediately, won't hold a shape.
  • Moist (Sticky): Forms a ball, leaves a little moisture on your palm.
  • Wet (Standing): Muddy, water actively drips when squeezed.

Reading the LCC:
  • Select the topmost fully expanded, healthy leaf.
  • Place the middle part of the leaf on the chart. Do not pluck it!
  • Block direct sunlight with your body while comparing the color.
  • Match the leaf color to the closest panel (1 to 6).

Perform this test only once to check germination success.
